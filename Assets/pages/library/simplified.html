How to Add Your Own Music

  • Copy one of the track templates below
  • Paste it in the "Your Tracks" section
  • Change the track title, artist, and duration
  • For album art, use an image URL or keep the default icon
  • Link to your audio file in the data-audio attribute

Add Your Music (HTML Template)

Copy and paste this HTML code in the "Your Tracks" section:

<div class="track-card" data-audio="YOUR_AUDIO_URL">
  <div class="album-art">
    <i class="fas fa-music"></i>
  </div>
  <div class="track-info">
    <div class="track-title">YOUR SONG TITLE</div>
    <div class="track-artist">YOUR ARTIST NAME</div>
    <div class="track-duration">3:45</div>
  </div>
  <div class="track-actions">
    <button class="play-btn" onclick="alert('Play: YOUR SONG TITLE')">
      <i class="fas fa-play"></i>
    </button>
  </div>
</div>
