OUR ORIGIN STORY

From a single artist's vision to a comprehensive promotional ecosystem, discover the journey that created Planet Play.
Early 2024

The Genesis

Planet Play was founded by Michael Edet as a personal initiative to elevate his own musical career. What began as a solo endeavor quickly revealed the universal challenges faced by artists in achieving visibility and professional recognition in an increasingly saturated digital landscape.

Mid 2024

Vision Expansion

Recognizing the broader application of his promotional strategies, Michael expanded Planet Play's mission to benefit fellow artists. The platform evolved into a collective promotional space, welcoming both established professionals and emerging talents seeking meaningful audience engagement.

Present Day

Digital-First Ecosystem

Today, Planet Play operates as a purely digital entity with no physical headquarters. Our presence is strategically concentrated on Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter), where we maintain active communities, provide artist support, and showcase success stories. This borderless approach allows us to serve artists globally.
