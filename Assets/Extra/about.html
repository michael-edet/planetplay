ABOUT PLANET PLAY

A revolutionary music promotion platform founded on the principle of elevating artistic concepts to professional dimensions through strategic digital placement and cross-platform amplification.

OUR FOUNDATIONAL STORY

From a personal initiative to a comprehensive promotional ecosystem, discover the journey that transformed Planet Play into a beacon for musical talent.

Planet Play emerged in early 2024 as a visionary initiative founded by Michael Edet, initially conceived as a strategic framework to elevate his own musical career. What began as a personal promotional endeavor quickly revealed the universal challenges faced by artists navigating an increasingly saturated digital landscape.

Recognizing the systemic barriers preventing talented musicians from achieving meaningful visibility, Michael expanded Planet Play's mission beyond personal advancement. The platform evolved into a collective promotional space dedicated to bridging the gap between emerging talent and established streaming ecosystems.

Our mission transcends mere playlist placement. We're building pathways to professional recognition that were previously inaccessible, transforming musical potential into sustainable streaming success.
— Michael Edet, Founder & CEO

Today, Planet Play operates as a purely digital entity with no physical headquarters, embodying the borderless nature of modern music consumption. Our strategic presence is concentrated across key social platforms where we maintain active communities, provide artist support, and showcase success stories of transformation through strategic digital placement.

KEY MILESTONES

Significant moments in our journey from personal initiative to established promotional platform.

Platform Inception

Planet Play founded by Michael Edet as a personal promotional initiative to elevate his musical career through strategic digital placement.

Mission Expansion
