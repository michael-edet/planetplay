Afroplanets brings together the best of African music, from Afrobeat to Highlife, Soukous to Amapiano. This playlist celebrates the rich musical heritage and contemporary sounds emerging from the continent.

Curated by our team of African music experts, Afroplanets is updated weekly with new releases and timeless classics that capture the spirit and energy of African music.
