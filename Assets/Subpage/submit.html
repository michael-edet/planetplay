Submission Requirements

  • High-Quality Audio Files

    Submit your tracks in WAV or high-quality MP3 format (320kbps). Ensure your audio is properly mixed and mastered.

  • Artist Information

    Provide your artist name, bio, social media links, and high-resolution profile picture.

  • Genre & Mood

    Specify your primary genre, sub-genre, and the mood/energy level of your track.

  • Artwork

    Include high-quality cover art (minimum 3000x3000 pixels) for your release.

  • Rights & Permissions

    Confirm you own all rights to the music or have permission to distribute it.
