ANNOUNCEMENT

New Platform Integration: SoundCloud Pro

November 15, 2023 5 min read By Planet Play Team

We are thrilled to announce a significant expansion of our platform offerings with the integration of SoundCloud Pro. This partnership marks a major milestone in our mission to provide artists with comprehensive distribution across all major streaming platforms.

Enhanced Distribution Capabilities

Starting today, artists using Planet Play can now distribute their music directly to SoundCloud Pro, gaining access to one of the world's largest music communities. This integration brings our total supported platforms to 9 major streaming services, solidifying our position as the most comprehensive music distribution platform available.

"The integration with SoundCloud Pro represents our commitment to providing artists with maximum reach and visibility. We believe every creator deserves access to all major platforms without barriers or complexity." - Planet Play CEO

Key Features of the Integration

Direct Upload & Distribution

Upload your tracks once through Planet Play and distribute them simultaneously to SoundCloud Pro alongside all other platforms. No additional accounts or uploads required.

Enhanced Analytics

Track your SoundCloud performance alongside all other platforms through our unified analytics dashboard. Get insights into plays, reposts, comments, and audience demographics.

Monetization Features

SoundCloud Pro's monetization features are now fully integrated. Eligible artists can earn revenue from their SoundCloud streams directly through Planet Play.

How It Benefits Artists

  • Simplified Workflow: Manage all your distribution from a single dashboard
  • Expanded Reach: Access SoundCloud's community of over 76 million active listeners
  • Unified Analytics: Compare performance across all platforms in one place
  • Streamlined Payments: Receive earnings from all platforms through a single payment system
  • Early Access Features: Be among the first to try new SoundCloud Pro features

Getting Started

Existing Planet Play artists can activate SoundCloud Pro distribution through their dashboard immediately. New users can select SoundCloud Pro as one of their distribution platforms during the signup process.

  1. Log in to your Planet Play artist dashboard
  2. Navigate to the "Platform Distribution" section
  3. Click "Connect SoundCloud Pro"
  4. Follow the simple authorization steps
  5. Your existing and future releases will be distributed automatically

There are no additional fees for this integration. SoundCloud Pro distribution is included in all Planet Play subscription plans.

Future Developments

This integration is just the beginning. We're already working on additional SoundCloud-specific features including advanced playlist pitching, direct-to-fan messaging tools, and enhanced repost networks. Stay tuned for more announcements in the coming months.
