ABOUT PLANET PLAY

A revolutionary music promotion platform developed and powered by Colony Core, dedicated to elevating artistic concepts to professional dimensions through strategic digital placement and technological innovation.

OUR FOUNDATIONAL STORY

Discover the unique journey of Planet Play, born from a fusion of musical passion and technological expertise through a groundbreaking partnership.

Planet Play represents a unique convergence of musical vision and technological innovation. Originally conceived by Michael Edet in early 2024 as a personal promotional initiative to elevate his musical career, the platform's potential quickly transcended individual aspirations.

Recognizing the universal challenges artists face in achieving digital visibility, Michael—a programmer by training—envisioned a more sophisticated solution. This vision led to a strategic collaboration with Ekemini Edet, forming a partnership that would transform Planet Play from a simple promotional concept into a comprehensive digital ecosystem powered by advanced software solutions.

Planet Play is more than just a music promotion platform—it's a technological ecosystem designed by programmers who understand both the art of music and the science of digital visibility.
— Michael Edet, Founder & Chief Architect

Today, Planet Play operates as a premier product of Colony Core, a software-based digital solution delivery platform dedicated to creating programmable solutions for clients across industries. This unique origin story ensures that every aspect of Planet Play is built on a foundation of technological excellence and strategic innovation.
